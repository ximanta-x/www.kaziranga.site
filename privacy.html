Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the Kaziranga Educational Platform handles information in connection with its educational and transparency functions.

Information Collection

This platform collects minimal information necessary for its educational and communication functions:

  • Contact Form Data: Information provided through the contact form (name, email, subject, message) for communication purposes
  • Automated Technical Data: Basic technical information (IP address, browser type, access times) for security and maintenance
  • Voluntary Communications: Information provided in formal written communications

Use of Information

Information collected is used strictly for:

  • Responding to communications and inquiries
  • Processing formal legal communications
  • Maintaining platform security and integrity
  • Improving educational content and documentation
  • Complying with applicable laws and due process

No information is used for commercial purposes or shared with third parties except as required by law.

Data Security

This platform implements appropriate security measures:

Server-Side Processing

All form submissions and data processing occur server-side via the /api/contact endpoint.

No Frontend Secrets

No API keys, bot tokens, or sensitive secrets are handled in the frontend code.

Minimal Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary for its intended purpose.

Legal Compliance

This platform operates in compliance with applicable privacy laws and regulations:

  • Information is processed lawfully and transparently
  • Data is collected for specific, legitimate purposes
  • Appropriate security measures are implemented
  • Information may be disclosed if required by law or legal process

Third-Party Services

This platform uses minimal third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (Roboto font)
  • Hosting Services: For platform availability

These services may have their own privacy policies governing data handling.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in platform operations or legal requirements. The latest update date will be indicated at the bottom of this page.

Contact for Privacy Concerns

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, or to exercise any applicable privacy rights, please use the Contact Form for formal written communication.

This platform is committed to addressing privacy concerns through proper communication channels.
