Educational Purpose & Intent

Primary Objectives

This platform exists as an independent, student-maintained website for educational documentation, learning, and transparency related to the Kaziranga region.

Public Transparency

To provide clear, accessible information in a structured format that promotes public understanding and transparency.

Educational Documentation

To create and maintain factual educational resources that serve learning and documentation purposes.

Prevention of Misinformation

To present information clearly and factually, reducing the potential for misunderstanding or misinformation.

Proper Legal Communication

To establish clear channels for formal communication and dispute resolution through proper written channels.

Scope & Limitations

This platform operates within specific boundaries to ensure clarity about its non-official status:

  • All content is strictly educational and documentary in nature
  • No commercial activities or monetization are conducted
  • No representation of any authority, institution, or administration is made
  • Geographical references are used descriptively, not authoritatively
  • Content is subject to correction or removal upon proper lawful communication

Cooperation & Due Process

This platform respects applicable laws, due process, and established procedures. We state clearly our cooperation with due process and encourage concerns to be raised through formal written channels for clear and responsible resolution.

The platform maintains a position of calm handling of disputes and transparent communication about its non-official status.

Documentation Context

The domain name (kaziranga.site) contains a geographical reference used strictly in a descriptive and educational sense. This website does not claim ownership, authority, representation, or official status over any protected area, institution, or administration.

Non-Official Status: This is an independent educational platform. No government endorsement implied or expressed.

Content hosted here is non-official, non-commercial, and subject to correction upon proper lawful communication.
